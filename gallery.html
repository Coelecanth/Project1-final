About Us
The fly fishing Lodge is a small Family business. We have a number of fly fishing lodges on the Kumquatcha River. The Kumquatcha River is about 100 miles north of Anchorage, where it empties in to the sea. The lodges are located along the river moving further in land. Alaskan fly fishing operates in co-operation with native Inuit Indians resident on the Kumquatcha River, and we operate our lodges on zero impact to the environment.
