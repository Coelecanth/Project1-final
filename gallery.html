Fly Fishing Lodge FAQ

Where great adventures await

The fly fishing Lodge is a small Family business, with the whole family involved Operated from Anchorage in Alaska USA for the last 25 years Giving fly fishers that once in a lifetime chance to visit the wilderness and fish in a truly wild and untouched enviornment, we pride our selves in operation a premium high quality, low impact destination for the Flyfisher, and always make sure out guest have a good time. and we have a number of fly fishing lodges on the Kumquatcha River. The Kumquatcha River is about 100 miles north of Anchorage where it empties in to the sea. The lodges are located along the river moving further in land. Alaskan fly fishing operates in co-operation with native Inuit Indians resident on the Kumquatcha River. We operate our lodges on zero impact to the environment,
