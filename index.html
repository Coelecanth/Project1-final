About Us

The fly fishing Lodge is a small family business, with the whole family involved; for the last 25 years. It is based from Anchorage in Alaska USA and we have a number of fly fishing lodges on the Kumquatcha River. We offer fly fishers that once in a lifetime chance to visit the wilderness and fish in a truly wild and untouched enviornment. We pride our selves in the operation of a premium, high quality, low impact destination for the Flyfisher. Alaskan fly fishing operates in co-operation with native Inuit Indians resident on the Kumquatcha River. We operate our lodges on zero impact to the environment.

We always aim to make sure our guests have a good time. But dont take our word for it, check out our reviews from customers on Facebook, Twitter, see some of customers adventures on Instagram.
