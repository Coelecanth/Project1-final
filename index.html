About Us

The Fly Fishing Lodge is a small family business, and for the last 25 years we have arranged fly fishing for fly fisherman on some of the best fishing in the world. We are based in Anchorage in Alaska USA. We offer fly fishers that once in a lifetime chance to visit the wilderness and fish in a truly wild and untouched environment. We pride ourselves in providing a premium destination, with some of the best fly fishing in the world.

We always aim to make sure our guests have a good time. But don't take our word for it, check out our reviews from customers on Facebook, Twitter, and see some of our customers adventures on Instagram.

As all our visits are very much about constructing a personalised trip to visit us at the Fly Fishing Lodge, please use our contact page and complete the form with details and we will be in touch to create your perfect fly fishing trip of a lifetime. The Fishing

Alaska is known as one of the top angling destinations in the world because of the incredible salmon runs of Chinook, Chum, Sockeye, and Arctic Char into the rivers. Combine this with numerous resident "Leopard" Rainbow Trout, Dolly Varden, and Grayling, and the fishing opportunities are enormous.

So whether you want to catch sheer numbers of fish, or looking for diverse angling opportunities or maybe that fish of lifetime Alaska has it all.

We cater exclusively for the fly fisherman at out lodges so you can be guaranteed to be guided by knowledgeable and competent fly fishers. Who have a vast amount of experience of the river and the fish and, how to catch them.

We cater for all ages and abilities of fly fisher, so whether you're a seasoned fly fisher or a complete beginner we can get you fishing and catching.
