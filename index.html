Alaskan fly fishing is a business operated from Acnhorage in Alaksa USA and we have a number of fly fishing lodges on the Kumquatcha River. The Kumquatcha River is about 100 miles north of Anchorage where it empties in to the sea. The best time to visit alaskan fly lodge is between the months of June and August during this time we have the salmon run, and also the native fish species in the river.
  • King, salmon, Pink Salmon, Chum Salmon, Sockeye Salmon
  • Grayling, rainbowe trout, arctic charr
